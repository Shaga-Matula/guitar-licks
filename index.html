Paul Gleeson has been playing acoustic & electric guitar for over 40 years and has been a member of the Registry Of Guitar Tutors and teaching for over 25 . Paul has worked in many band projects with style’s such as Punk, Ska , Rock’n Roll, Jazz , Blues and Rock. Over the years and has recorded an album and published a book on the subject.
Paul’s strength lies in teaching both playing the guitar and music theory.

Ethos

"Music creates order out of chaos: for rhythm imposes unanimity upon the divergent, melody imposes continuity upon the disjointed, and harmony imposes compatibility upon the incongruous."
Yehudi Menuhin


We teach people of all ages from age 3 to 103 and above if we can. A structured and comprehensive course is laid out for beginner, intermediate and advanced players. Classes last 30 min and can be group or private one to one tuition if so desired. We can also tailor classes to suit the individual such as the junior certificate, leaving certificate or college degree.
We are registered with the "Registry of Guitar Tutors" and are qualified to teach and hold the exams on site for students form grades 1 to 8.
We teach music theory which can be taken as grades and is accredited to the "London College of Music"
We also have a band camp which is optional but free as we just love to play. The goal of band camp is to boost confidence and provide students with the tools to preforme in a live envoirament. Whilst this paticular aspect of our teaching is optional we do encourage students to take part for the "crack" and also invualuable experience. After all music is meant for sharing.
So come join us
